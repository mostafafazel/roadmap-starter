## Text Styles

- **Bold**: `**Bold**` or `__Bold__`
- *Italic*: `*Italic*` or `_Italic_`
- ***Bold and Italic***: `***Bold and Italic***` or `___Bold and Italic___`
- ~~Strikethrough~~: `~~Strikethrough~~`

## Lists

### Unordered List
- Item 1
- Item 2
  - Subitem 1
  - Subitem 2

### Ordered List
1. First item
2. Second item
   1. Subitem 1
   2. Subitem 2
